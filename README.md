## 🕹️ How to Play

### Step 1 — Connect Wallet
- Install **MetaMask** (or any EVM wallet)
- Switch to **Monad Mainnet** · Chain ID: `143`
- RPC: `https://rpc.monad.xyz`

### Step 2 — Choose Your Character
- 🔵 **USDC Rider** — RARE
- 🟣 **ETH Warrior** — EPIC
- 🟢 **Monad Racer** — LEGENDARY

### Step 3 — Spin the Wheel
- Hit **Spin** for a lucky start
- Pay **1 MON** to mint your card NFT

### Step 4 — Scratch Your Card
- Scratch the card canvas to reveal your prize tier

### Step 5 — Claim Your Prize
- If you win → the prize is sent to your wallet **instantly**
